TRUE! PIGS ARE SUFFERING

Did you know that thousands of pigs in this country are suffering? Many people don't realize the kind of conditions that pigs have to live in to keep up with the demand for cheap meat.

Sows have a gestation period of 3 months, 3 weeks, and 3 days for a total of 114 days. The use of farrowing or gestation crates is very common. They cannot turn around or walk. This is animal cruelty.

They keep pigs in these tiny boxes for years, having back to back pregnancies/births.
The local pig farmers I've spoken to argue that it is to prevent piglet deaths (it is not uncommon for the sow to accidentally roll ontop of her newborn babies to try to nurse them.) I have successfully raised a piglet litter without the use of a farrowing crate by simply allowing the pigs enough room.
Boycott cheap pork/pork!
